ABOUT US

No organization survives without a strong
ground holding them tight. From the start,
we will always grow by these core values.

ORGANIZATION VALUES

Mission

Our mission is to respond to emergencies protecting people, property,
the city of Paranaque, and its nearby cities fostering a responsible team
approach planning, education, and hazard prevention with a dedicated,
professionally trained, and compassionate volunteer workforce.

Vision

Our vision is to create a progressive volunteer organization unified in
creating a safe and secure community that will impact people’s lives by
striving for excellence and being recognized as the premiere Fire-EMS-
Rescue volunteer in the city.

How to volunteer?

What are the minimum requirements I need to
meet to be a volunteer firefighter

  • Applicant volunteer must be at least 18 years old
    and above.
  • Must be physically and mentally fit.
  • Must undergo screening/interview with the officers.

    • Must submit the following:

  • Endorsement letter from old brigade. (For volunteer
    who wish to  be transferred to PFCFVA.) 
  • Barangay Clearance
  • Updated resume with picture.
